Help
🔐 Sign Up / Sign In
Sign Up — enter your name + create a 4-digit PIN. Sign In — enter your name + same PIN to load your data on any device.
🏠 Home
See today's tasks, classes and days to JEE. Tap any day in the weekly calendar to see that day's lectures.
✅ Tasks
Study tab → Tasks. Type a task, tap + Add. Tap a task to mark done.
⏱ Study Timer
Study tab → Study Tracker. Pick a subject, tap Start/Stop to log session time.
🤖 AI Assistant
Use the AI tab. Ask about your tasks, study time, days left to JEE, attendance, syllabus progress — or any JEE concept from Physics, Chemistry or Maths. Works fully offline!
🎨 Settings
Menu (|||) → Settings. Change theme colour, toggle sound, adjust volume.
☁️ Sync
Data saves to cloud automatically every 5 min. Use the same PIN on any device to access your data.
